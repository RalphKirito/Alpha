What is YouthHack?

YouthHack is a nonprofit organization that was started back in June 2014 with the goal of helping high school students learn more about technology and entrepreneurship. YouthHack started out as a two-day technopreneurship challenge event in Manila, Philippines where students got a chance to listen to actual entrepreneurs and venture capitalists. With the help of ideation, prototyping and pitching workshops, students also got a chance to showcase their ideas to a panel of judges.

SEE ACTIVITIES
MISSION

YouthHack empowers students to turn their ideas into reality through experimental programs that help students build their skills and apply them to impactful projects.

VISION

YouthHack will be the go-to place for students to learn more about startups, technology and entrepreneurship, and the platform for them to kickstart their startup journeys.

WHY WE EXIST

Our core purpose is to educate young individuals about the world of startups, technology and entrepreneurship, and to inspire them to use their skills and resources to create products and solutions that can solve today's pressing problems.

INSPIRE

We bring in experienced entrepreneurs and venture capitalists to share their stories and inspire students to step out of their comfort zone and take action.

EDUCATE

We hold different types of workshops for students to learn more about ideation, product development, marketing, growth hacking, strategic design and building a startup.

EMPOWER

We empower students by giving them the opportunity to pitch their ideas to venture capitalists, CEOs and experienced entrepreneurs and get feedback on how to improve their ideas.

EXPOSE

We expose students to the world of startups and entrepreneurship by connecting them with startups from all over their area.
