Six Steps to Pitch Perfectly
By YouthHack Manila
July 23, 2015

Just two weeks ago, some of Manila’s best student innovators hacked their way to the top at our first Code Weekend. This weekend, watch out for future gamechangers in the business industry. Are you ready? Here are a few quick tips to get your head in the game.

1. Step up your KISSing game. Keep it short and simple, and go straight to the heart of the matter. In sixty seconds or less, try to answer the following:

What is your product trying to solve?
Who is your target market?
What can your startup offer that nothing else can?
What is your competitive advantage?
Is there enough room for growth?

2. Demo your product, if you can. Show them what it does, and what it can do in the future.

3. Hook, line, and sinker. Capture your audience with your first word! Be witty. Be punny. Be confident. Your product is interesting, so why shouldn’t you be, too?

4. You’re selling an idea, a lifestyle, a future. Clearly define your revenue model. Project your startup’s growth.

5. Be a social medium, and communicate with your target market. Show potential investors that people ARE interested in whatever you’re doing. Conduct surveys. Invite your friends to like your Facebook page. Hustle and get your brand out there.

6. Finally, back your pitch with a solid and sustainable business plan. In the end, business is still business, and this is no time to monkey around. Remember that the best pitches aren’t always the funniest or those with the most data. They’re the ones that investors think can deliver in six months (or more) what they promised in sixty seconds.
