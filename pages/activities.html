CODE WEEKEND
The Code Weekend is intended for both high school and university students. This 36-hour programming marathon aims to equip them with skills necessary for web design and basic application development. On the last day of the event, they will present their work in a panel of judges. YH CALABARZON will launch its Code Weekend on March 12-13 in Los Baños, Laguna.

YouthHack's signature two day event, StartUp Challenge, brought together hundreds of high school and college students to learn more about technology and entrepreneurship. Students go through workshops and come up with their own ideas before pitching them to a panel of actual investors.
