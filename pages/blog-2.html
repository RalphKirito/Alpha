Six Steps to Pitch Perfectly
By YouthHack Manila
July 23, 2015

Just two weeks ago, some of Manila’s best student innovators hacked their way to the top at our first Code Weekend. This weekend, watch out for future gamechangers in the business industry. Are you ready? Here are a few quick tips to get your head in the game. 1. Step up your KISSing game. Keep it short and simple, and go straight to the heart of the matter. In sixty seconds or less, try to answer the following: What is your product trying to solve? Who is your target market? What can your startup offer that nothing else can? What is your competitive advantage? Is there enough room for growth?
